Frequently Asked Questions

What is the PWRT Token?

PWRT is a reflection token that is designed to generate passive THOR rewards for you, by rewarding PWRT holders with THOR tokens. This means that you will be able to take advantage of daily THOR rewards.

Is there a minimum amount of PWRT Tokens required for rewards?

If you would like to benefit from the THOR reward distribution you will have to hold at least 250 $PWRT tokens.

How often are rewards paid out?

Our innovative reward system is triggered by volume (buy + sell activity). Payouts to holders may not always occur every hour if trading volume is low. When this is the case, the amount of rewarded THOR each holder will receive does not decrease. However, will accrue over a longer period of time until it is suitable for a payout to trigger (usually only takes a few hours).

How do the fees work?

For every buy and sell of the PWRT token 7% goes to THOR rewards, 5% goes to the liquidity pool and 1% to the protocol marketing wallet. Our innovative protocol is meant to reward long term hodlers. Fees do not apply for THOR rewards.

Why hasn't the team doxxed themselves yet?

We understand the stigma in the crypto market when it comes to trust and projects rugpulling their investors. KYC with assurance is on the top of the to do list. As well as a contract audit by Certik.

How will the project remain sustainable long term and how will the project survive the bear market?

The important question of sustainability remains a high priority. We will be adding high yield staking/farming to reduce sell pressure. As well as added benefits for our community stakers such as early access to NFT's, Free Airdrops that come along, as well as soon to be community voting rights. PowerThor does not aim to be a quick flip for profit for day traders. We aim to have long-term growth and sustainablity to change the lives of our loyal community. As far as the topic of the bear market, we will be investing in high-yield defi-protocols that aren't going away anytime soon. As well as having reserves in place to survive the bear market.
